# AIPP Capstone Project Assessment Criteria

Please ensure that your project and presentation align with the following AIPP Capstone Project Assessment Criteria:

## 1. Problem Framing
* Clearly define the problem being addressed.
* Demonstrate that the problem is real, specific, and suitable for an AI-based solution.
* Ensure the project scope is appropriate for the available time and resources.

## 2. Technical Execution
* Present a functional codebase, prototype, or AI artefact.
* Demonstrate the quality of prompts used (where applicable).
* Provide a deployment, demonstration, or working proof of concept.
* Show appropriate use of AI tools, Python, and related technologies.

## 3. AI Ethics and Governance
* Include an AI governance structure.
* Identify potential risks such as bias, privacy, and data protection concerns.
* Describe mitigation measures, including both technical and organisational controls.
* Document explainability considerations and responsible AI design decisions.

## 4. Integration of Learning
* Demonstrate the application of at least three course modules.
* Clearly explain how concepts from different modules have been integrated within the project.

## 5. Communication
* Deliver a clear, well-structured, and professional presentation.
* Ensure the written report is concise, coherent, and well-organized.
* Communicate your ideas confidently and effectively.

## 6. Reflection
* Provide an honest evaluation of the project.
* Discuss what worked well, challenges encountered, project limitations, and lessons learned.
* Highlight what you would do differently in future iterations.

Please prepare accordingly and ensure your presentation effectively demonstrates both the technical and strategic aspects of your project.
